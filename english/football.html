Football Pitch

The Pitch

A rectangular grass field, 100-110m long and 64-75m wide, with goals at each end.

Penalty Area

The 18-yard box where goalkeepers can use hands and fouls result in penalty kicks.

Center Circle

A 10-yard radius circle where kickoffs occur. Opponents must stay outside during kickoff.

Game Rules

Essential rules every football fan should know

  • Each team has 11 players including one goalkeeper. Matches are 90 minutes (two 45-minute halves).
  • The objective is to score more goals than the opponent by getting the ball into their goal.
  • Players cannot touch the ball with hands/arms (except goalkeeper in penalty area).
  • Offside: An attacking player cannot be nearer to the goal than the second-last defender when receiving the ball.
  • A foul inside the penalty area results in a penalty kick from 12 yards.
  • Yellow card is a warning; two yellows or a direct red card means ejection.
  • Throw-ins occur when the ball crosses the sideline; corner kicks or goal kicks when crossing the goal line.
  • The referee can add stoppage time for delays during play.
  • VAR (Video Assistant Referee) reviews goals, penalties, red cards, and mistaken identity.
  • Teams can make up to 5 substitutions in most competitions.
