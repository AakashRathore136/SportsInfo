Basketball Court

The Court

A rectangular hardwood court measuring 94 feet long and 50 feet wide with baskets at each end.

Three-Point Line

A curved line 23.75 feet from the basket. Shots made beyond this line score 3 points.

Key/Paint

The rectangular area near the basket where fouls are committed while shooting and free throws occur.

Game Rules

Essential rules every basketball fan should know

  • Each team has 5 players on the court. NBA games are 48 minutes (four 12-minute quarters).
  • The objective is to score more points than the opponent by shooting the ball through the basket.
  • A field goal scores 2 points; a three-pointer scores 3 points; a free throw scores 1 point.
  • A team gets possession after a basket and the opposing team inbounds the ball.
  • Teams have 24 seconds (NBA) to attempt a shot or lose possession.
  • Traveling occurs when a player moves more than 1.5 steps without dribbling the ball.
  • Double dribble is when a player dribbles, stops, then dribbles again with the same hand.
  • Fouls are infractions; a player is fouled out after 6 fouls in the NBA.
  • Personal fouls can result in free throws for the opponent if they are in the penalty.
  • A defensive 3-second violation occurs when a defender stays in the key without guarding anyone.
